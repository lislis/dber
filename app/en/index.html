The usergroup

The most frequently asked question: What do we actually do at our meetups?

Usually we sit together and talk about diaspora* and related topics. That can be news from the community or what's happening in development. But also other open source software or hat people did since the last meetup.

We are open to everything and everyone. It's a usergroup after all and users are the front and center. So don't be shy and visit us some time :)

About dber

Everything started in late 2011 with the hashtag #diasporaBerlin that the Berlin based users of diaspora* used to meet every month at the c-base hacksapce. Near the end of 2012 we moved to our current host IN-Berlin where smokers have to go outside.

In 2014 we bought a domain and set up this page for the Berlin community. More things to come!

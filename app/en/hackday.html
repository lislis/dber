What? and How?

Diaspora* is a decentralized social network. The software is open source, which means not only that everybody is allowed to use it, but also that everybody is allowed to work on it. Sadly not a lot of people do that.

This hackday aims to change that. Starting something is hard and that's why we try to make this start as enjoyable as possible: together with like-minded people.

We'll meet this Saturday and take a look at how to set up diaspora* locally, what issues to work on as beginners, how to fix bugs and how to submit the perfect pull request. Along the way we'll have the wiki and IRC open and work our way through.

Everybody is welcome, no matter the skill level. The important thing is that you're interested in diaspora* and want to take a look at some code.
